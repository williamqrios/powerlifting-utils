A set of useful calculators for the sport of powerlifting.

We currently have the following calculators:

  • Plate Calculator: easily figure out how many plates you need to load on the barbell for your working sets, based on how many plates you have available at your disposal.
  • Estimated 1 Repetition Maximum (E1RM) Calculator: determine an approximation of your 1RM according to your repetition capacity, weight lifted, repetitions performed and rating of perceived exertion (RPE).

If you have any more suggestions or encounter any bugs, feel free to open an issue on the project's GitHub page.
